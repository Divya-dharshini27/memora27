# 📖 Memora

> *Preserve the stories that matter most — before they're gone forever.*

A nostalgic web app to record, store, and revisit precious life memories through voice, photos, and documents — with an AI chatbot to explore them.

---

## ✨ Features
- 🎙 Voice recording in the browser
- 📷 Photo & document uploads
- 🏷 Emotion tags (happy, nostalgic, proud...)
- 🔍 Search by keyword, emotion, or date
- 💬 AI chatbot to explore your memories
- 🔐 Secure login with Supabase Auth
